About Us

Vextro Industries was established in 2010, quickly becoming a trusted name in the hardware and sanitary fittings industry. Our mission is to deliver innovative, reliable, and aesthetically pleasing solutions for modern infrastructure projects.

We serve both domestic and international markets with our quality products and services. Our company is rooted in a tradition of excellence and a commitment to customer satisfaction. We take pride in developing a wide range of products, including hinges, door handles, sanitary fittings, and bath accessories, all backed by our expertise and dedication to quality.

Mission

To support our customers by providing superior products and services of exceptional value which help them gain competitive advantage in their markets.

Vision

To sustain our vision by constantly seeking renewal via continual improvement in processes, and the application of new technologies and best business practices.

Core Value

To continuously improve our products & services, we believe that a perfect understanding of the clients need is of crucial importance and also adhere to the concept of corporate social responsibility.
